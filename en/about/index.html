Theme

This theme, Astro-Axi, is heavily modified based on Astro's Pure, Cactus, and Resume themes. The modifications include decoupling components from packages and moving them into the Astro-conventional `components` directory to fully support hot reloading; redesigning the styles of many components, rewriting pages such as the Tags page, and adding a Publication interface; fixing numerous bugs, such as incorrect calculations in the BackToTop component; and adding support for i18n multi-language functionality.
